{{< content-modules/section-header title="Understanding 10K Gold and Its Value" level="2" >}}
  Before calculating the worth of 8 grams of 10K gold, it's important to understand what 10K gold actually is and how gold purity affects value.
{{< /content-modules/section-header >}}

Gold purity is measured in karats, with 24K representing 100% pure gold. 10K gold contains 10 parts gold out of 24 parts total, making it 41.7% pure gold. The remaining 58.3% consists of other metals like copper, silver, nickel, or zinc that add durability and can change the color of the gold.

![A close-up image of 10K gold markings on jewelry](https://ik.imagekit.io/appraisily/SEO/how-much-is-8-grams-of-10k-gold-worth/how-much-is-8-grams-of-10k-gold-worth-image-2_lSyfFWMjA.png)

10K gold is the minimum karat that can legally be sold as gold in the United States. It's commonly used in everyday jewelry because it's:

1. More affordable than higher karat options
2. More durable and scratch-resistant
3. Less likely to cause allergic reactions than gold-plated items

While 10K gold has the lowest gold content among commonly sold gold jewelry, it still holds significant value due to its gold content. Let's look at how that value is calculated.

{{< content-modules/section-header title="How to Calculate the Value of 8 Grams of 10K Gold" level="2" >}}
  The value of 10K gold depends on the current market price of gold and the amount of pure gold content in your item.
{{< /content-modules/section-header >}}

To calculate the value of 8 grams of 10K gold, follow these steps:

1. Determine the current spot price of gold per gram
2. Calculate the gold content percentage (10K = 41.7% or 0.417)
3. Multiply: Weight × Purity × Spot Price

For example, if gold is trading at $87 per gram:

```
8 grams × 0.417 purity × $87 per gram = $290.26
```

This calculation gives you the "melt value" or intrinsic gold value of your 10K gold item. However, the actual buying and selling prices will vary from this base value.

Gold prices fluctuate daily based on global economic factors, including:
