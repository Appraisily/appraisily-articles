### Written Certified Appraisals

The core service provided by Molchany is professionally written, certified appraisals of personal property. These detailed documents are essential for:

- Insurance coverage and claims
- Estate planning and probate
- Charitable donation tax deductions
- Divorce settlements
- Personal knowledge and collection management

Each appraisal follows industry standards and can be used for official purposes, providing clients with legally recognized documentation of their items' value.

### Estate Liquidation Services

For clients dealing with entire estates, Molchany offers comprehensive liquidation services that include:

- Complete inventory assessment
- Accurate valuation of all items
- Organization and execution of estate sales
- Consignment arrangements for select pieces
- Coordination with auction houses when appropriate

This turnkey approach allows executors and heirs to handle estate settlement efficiently while maximizing the return on valuable items.

### Real Estate Services

Leveraging their dual expertise, Molchany combines antique appraisal with real estate services, offering:

- Property valuation that considers both real estate and contents
- Coordinated sale of property and possessions
- Specialized marketing for historic or unique properties
- Guidance through the entire process of property liquidation

This integrated approach is particularly valuable for clients managing estate settlements or major life transitions.
