## Department 56 Identification Guide

Properly identifying Department 56 pieces is essential for accurate valuation. Each piece contains specific markings that provide valuable information about its production history and authenticity.

![Department 56 markings and identification features closeup](https://ik.imagekit.io/appraisily/SEO/department-56-value-guide-2022/department-56-value-guide-2022-image-5_rbJ7mgDIa.png)

### Understanding Department 56 Markings

Department 56 pieces include several identifying features:

1. **Bottom Stamp**: Look for the Department 56 name, collection name, and copyright date
2. **Production Code**: Numbers indicating manufacturing information
3. **Special Markings**: "Retired" stamps, limited edition numbering, or event designations
4. **Artist Signatures**: Some special pieces include designer signatures

### Dating Department 56 Pieces

Determining the production year is crucial for valuation. Here's how to interpret Department 56 date codes:

1. **Copyright Date**: The year shown after Â© indicates the design date, not necessarily the production year
2. **Production Date**: Often follows the copyright date
3. **Box Codes**: Original packaging often contains date codes printed on the bottom
4. **Catalogs and Guides**: Compare your piece with official Department 56 catalogs to confirm production years

### Spotting Reproductions and Fakes

The market value of Department 56 has unfortunately attracted counterfeiters. Be wary of:

1. **Inconsistent Markings**: Authentic Department 56 pieces have clear, consistent markings
2. **Poor Paint Quality**: Authentic pieces feature hand-painted details with consistent quality
3. **Incorrect Materials**: Department 56 buildings are made from high-quality porcelain
4. **Packaging Discrepancies**: Original boxes have specific printing characteristics and materials
5. **Price Too Good To Be True**: Significantly underpriced rare pieces are often reproductions

## Collecting Strategies for 2022

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting, these strategies can help guide your Department 56 collecting in 2022.

### Investment-Focused Collecting

For those primarily interested in value appreciation:

1. **Focus on Rarity** - Pieces with limited production runs tend to appreciate faster
2. **Condition is Critical** - Only purchase mint condition items with original packaging
3. **Research Production Numbers** - Lower production pieces typically become more valuable
4. **Complete Sets** - Building complete themed sets often provides better returns than random pieces
5. **Documentation** - Maintain certificates, receipts, and provenance information

### New Department 56 Releases for 2022

The new releases for 2022 worth considering for long-term value:

1. **Anniversary Edition Pieces** - Commemorative pieces typically become sought after
2. **Event Exclusives** - Only available to event attendees or through specific retailers
3. **Themed Sets** - Complete new thematic groupings with accessories
4. **Final Series Pieces** - Last pieces in retiring series often appreciate quickly

### Preservation and Display Tips

Proper care significantly impacts long-term value:

1. **Clean Carefully** - Use only soft brushes and avoid liquid cleaners
2. **Control Environment** - Store in climate-controlled spaces away from direct sunlight
3. **Handle Minimally** - Always hold buildings by the base, never by delicate features
4. **Secure Display** - Use museum putty or similar products to prevent falls
5. **Rotate Storage** - If not displaying year-round, repack carefully in original materials
