## Authentication Techniques  
Experts use a 7-point verification system:  

**1. Material Analysis**  
- Pre-1940 mohair shows irregular weave patterns under 10x magnification [9]  
- Wood wool stuffing indicates pre-1920 construction [12]  

**2. Label Examination**  
- Steiff buttons transitioned from nickel (1904-1914) to aluminum (post-WWII) [9]  
- 1920s Chiltern labels used specific serif fonts [13]  

**3. Construction Details**  
- Hand-stitched seams (3-5 stitches per inch) vs machine stitching (8-12 stitches) [12]  
- Glass eyes pre-1950s have slight surface imperfections [14]  

**4. Wear Patterns**  
- Authentic patina shows even fading on friction points (elbows, muzzle) [1]
