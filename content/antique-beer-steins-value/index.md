## 5. Authentication Guide  
**Authentic Traits**:  
- **Lids**: Single-cast pewter with thumb lever; interior lighter than exterior[9][12]  
- **Markings**: "Geschützt" (patented) or "Germany" (post-1892)[3][14]  
- **Materials**: Hand-carved stoneware vs modern resin replicas[11]  

**Red Flags**:  
- Multi-piece fused lids[12]  
- Uniform patina (indicates chemical aging)[11]  
- "Made in West Germany" stamps (post-1949)[14]
