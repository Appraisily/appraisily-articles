**Sterling silver** (92.5% pure silver) forms the baseline of value, with melt value calculated as:  
`(Weight in troy ounces) × (0.925) × (Current silver spot price)` [8][15].

- **Rarity**: Complete 19th-century sets gain 30-50% premium over mismatched pieces [1][9]

## 5. Authentication Techniques  
**Hallmark Analysis**:  
- British sterling: Lion passant + date letter [4]  
- French sterling: Rooster + Minerva head [4]  

**Scientific Testing**:  
- XRF analysis: Non-destructive purity verification
