## Historical Development of Carolina Mirrors  
The Carolina Mirror Company, founded in 1923 in Raleigh, North Carolina, began as a manufacturer of sheet mirrors for furniture before expanding into decorative wall mirrors[3][8]. Under the ownership of the Cherokee Indian Nation (1980s–2000s) and later Sun Capital Partners, the company invested in advanced techniques like acid etching and hand-silvering to create intricate designs[3]. Notable collaborations include the *Biltmore Estate Collection* and *Court of Versailles Collection*, which reproduced historical motifs for modern applications[3]. By the mid-20th century, Carolina mirrors became synonymous with high-end residential and commercial interiors, adorning spaces in hotels and luxury homes[3][5].
