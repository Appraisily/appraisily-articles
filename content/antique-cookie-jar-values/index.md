## 5. Authentication Guide  
**Step 1: Examine Markings**  
- McCoy: "USA" stamp with crescent moon (1940s) or "McCoy" script [9][13]  
- American Bisque: Raised "AB" or paper labels [1]  
- Regal China: Chicago origin stamps [5]  

**Step 2: Assess Construction**  
- Weight: Authentic McCoy jars weigh 4-7 lbs vs 2-3 lbs for fakes [9][13]  
- Glaze: Semi-gloss finish without cloudiness [9]  
- Mold Lines: Sharp details vs blurred reproductions [9]  

**Step 3: Cross-Reference**  
- Consult McCoy Pottery Collectors' Society databases  
- Compare to verified auction results [6][10]

## 8. Preservation Best Practices  
1. **Cleaning**:  
   - Undamaged jars: Mild soap + soft brush  
   - Cracked jars: Vinegar/water solution wipe [7]  
2. **Storage**:  
   - Line shelves with felt padding  
   - Avoid temperature fluctuations  
3. **Display**:  
   - Rotate every 6 months to prevent glaze wear  
   - UV-filtered glass cabinets [7][13]
